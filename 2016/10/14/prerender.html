Testing your middleware:

Prerender adheres to google’s _escaped_fragment_ proposal, which we recommend you use. It’s easy:

  • Just add <meta name="fragment" content="!"> to the of all of your pages
  • If you use hash urls (#), change them to the hash-bang (#!)
  • That’s it! Perfect SEO on javascript pages.

Then access your site:
http://www.example.com?_escaped_fragment_=

If your URLs look like this:
http://www.example.com/user/1
Then access your URLs like this:
http://www.example.com/user/1?_escaped_fragment_=

If you use the hashbang (#!):
If your URLs look like this:
http://www.example.com/#!/user/1
Then access your URLs like this:
http://www.example.com/?_escaped_fragment_=/user/1

If you have your middleware set up correctly, you should see your page returned! You’ll know it’s correct if you view the source of your page and see all of the HTML in there instead of just the JavaScript.
